Facts About Doraemon

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century who was sent back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi. Unlike normal robots, Doraemon has emotions, friendships, and a strong sense of responsibility. He carries a four-dimensional pocket that can store futuristic gadgets, which he uses to help Nobita solve problemsâ€”often leading to unexpected trouble. Doraemon is afraid of mice, despite being a robot, and loves dorayaki (sweet red bean pancakes).

Doraemon was created by Fujiko F. Fujio and first appeared in 1969. Although he looks like a blue cat, he originally had ears that were eaten by a robot mouse, which caused his fear of mice. Doraemon has become a cultural icon in Japan and many other countries, representing friendship, imagination, and hope for a better future through technology.
